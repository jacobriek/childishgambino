    "Awaken, My Love!" is the third studio album by American hip hop artist Childish Gambino, released on December 2, 2016 by Glassnote Records.

    The album's release was preceded by two lead singles, "Me and Your Mama" and "Redbone."

    Consisting of tracks largely sung rather than rapped, its retro fusion of psychedelic soul, funk and R&B influences was considered a bold departure from the predominately hip hop style of his prior work.
